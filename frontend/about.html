Our Journey

How LHS Grew

From a small idea to India's most trusted legal hiring platform — here's how we got here.

2018
The Idea

LHS was conceived by a group of legal professionals and tech entrepreneurs frustrated by the lack of transparency in finding qualified lawyers.

2019
First 100 Lawyers

We launched with a curated directory of 100 verified lawyers across Delhi, Mumbai, and Bangalore.

2021
Pan-India Expansion

Expanded to 30+ cities with 250+ verified lawyers, covering major High Courts and sessions courts.

2024
500+ Professionals

Today, LHS serves thousands of clients monthly with a network of 500+ rigorously verified legal professionals.
