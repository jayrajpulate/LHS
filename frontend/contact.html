How does LHS verify lawyers?
Every lawyer listed on LHS goes through a multi-step verification process - including Bar Council registration check, credential review, and client reference validation. Only approved professionals are listed publicly.
Is there a fee to use LHS as a client?
No. Browsing and contacting lawyers through LHS is completely free for clients. You only pay the lawyer directly for their legal services - we don't take a commission from clients.
How can I list my practice on LHS?
Lawyers and advocates can apply to be listed by contacting us at info@example.com or using the contact form above with Subject: List My Services. Our team will review your credentials and respond within 2–3 business days.
Is my personal information safe?
Absolutely. LHS takes privacy seriously. We never sell or share your personal data with third parties. All communications are encrypted and your case details remain strictly confidential.
What areas of law does LHS cover?
LHS covers 30+ practice areas including Criminal Law, Family Law, Corporate Law, Property & Real Estate, Civil Litigation, IP Law, Tax Law, Labour Law, and many more. You can filter by practice area on our Find Lawyers page.
